Official letters of Invitation Letter for application of Visa at Vietnam Embassy will be available to REGISTERED and PAID participants only. These letters do not and cannot commit the Organizing Committee to any financial obligation. Upon successful completion of your online registration and full payment of your registration fee, please contact sii_registration@ml.jaist.ac.jp with the paper ID in RAS system , the full name of invited person and passport number to request your visa invitation letter.
Registration can only be confirmed once payment is received. Payment can be made by any of the following methods:
  • - Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • - Wire payment
To request a refund, please send an email to sii_registration@ml.jaist.ac.jp. Please note that all cancellations need to be made before December 1, 2023, 11:59pm EST, and will be subjected to a US$100.00 cancellation fee. Refunds for cancellation requests will only be considered in exceptional circumstances. For Full Registration, and if your co-authors also do not register, please be aware that your paper submission will also be canceled if you request a refund. Nonetheless, the paper with only one registration withdrawn after Camera Ready upload will not be considered for refund. It's important to understand that failure to obtain the necessary visa to travel to Vietnam does not qualify for a refund.
