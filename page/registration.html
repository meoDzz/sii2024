Note:

Paper Uploads: To submit the final paper and all associated attachments, at least one of the authors must register in advance (see above). Authors who pay a non-student registration fee (excludes one-day registrations) are allowed to upload up to 2 contributions, and all other registrations are limited to one paper and one late breaking new or demonstration upload (excludes one-day registrations). Papers should be six pages (US letter size) in length, with up to two extra pages ($205 USD charge per extra page). The number of pages includes the references, appendices, etc. Registrations cannot be shared or used for uploading a paper for which the registrant is not an author.
