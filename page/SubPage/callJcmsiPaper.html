Call for JCMSI paper

Templates and Submission Procedure

We are pleased to announce that we will accept JCMSI (SICE Journal of Control, Measurement, and System Integration) regular papers, other than survey papers, to be presented (oral) at SII (IEEE/SICE International Symposium on System Integration) without any further review processes, starting from SII 2024. Note that such papers will not be included in proceedings of SII 2024, since they are already published on JCMSI.

  • Eligibility: Papers, which has been published on-line between 1 July, 2022 to 30 June, 2023.
  • Application deadline: 1 August, 2024 through PaperPlaza system .
  • Other condition: You must complete full registration with paper before the normal registration deadline.
Submission Procedure

Please to fill in this application form and submit it through the IEEE PaperPlaza Conference Management System then click to Submit a contribution to SII 2024 , from which you will see JCMSI submission and clik Submit . From here, please follow neccessary steps for submitting the Application Form
