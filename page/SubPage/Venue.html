The 2024 IEEE/SICE International Symposium on System Integration
Ha Long, Vietnam 8-11th January, 2024

Venue

Halong Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and popular travel destination in Quang Ninh Province, Vietnam. The name Ha Long means "descending dragon". Administratively, the bay belongs to Ha Long city, Cam Pha city, and is a part of Van Don district. The bay features thousands of limestone karsts and isles in various shapes and sizes. Ha Long Bay is a center of a larger zone which includes Bai Tu Long Bay to the northeast, and Cat Ba Island to the southwest. These larger zones share a similar geological, geographical, geomorphological, climate, and cultural characters.

Vinpearl Resort & Spa Ha Long

SII 2024 will be taken place at Vinpearl Resort and Spa in Halong, nestled on Dao Reu Island, a mere few hundred meters from the coastline.

As the first & only resort in the North of Vietnam built entirely on the sea, Vinpearl Resort & Spa Ha Long makes an unforgettable impression with 3 private beaches, green premises and an outdoor swimming pool of up to 1,200m2. Especially, the system of spacious rooms is exquisitely designed with large balconies - ideal for waking up on the wonder bay with the beautiful Ha Long city in distance. Just an hour drive from Hanoi, Vinpearl Resort & Spa Ha Long is the ideal choice to enjoy a weekend getaway with family, an impromptu "pack your suitcase and go" trip, or a classy meeting combined with holiday marking success.
